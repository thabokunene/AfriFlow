The value of AfriFlow comes from its ability to retain
customers and create a sustainable competitive moat.
This document describes the retention levers and where
moats are formed.

## Retention Levers

- **Integrated intelligence**: cross domain insights
  that are hard for competitors to replicate.
- **Embedded workflows**: signals delivered into the
  relationship management workflows.
- **Network effects**: the more clients on the
  platform, the richer the signal set.

## Moat Sources

1. **Data scale**: Aggregate view of clients across
   five domains and multiple countries.
2. **Proprietary signals**: derived metrics that are
   not available outside the platform.
3. **Operational embedding**: integrations with credit
   decision engines, CRM, and trading systems.

## Metrics

Monitor these to validate moat strength:

- Signal adoption rate
- Time to value for new clients
- Churn reduction compared to baseline
- Share of wallet captured
