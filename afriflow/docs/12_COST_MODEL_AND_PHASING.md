# 12 Cost Model and Phasing

> **Disclaimer**: Please read
> [DISCLAIMER.md](../DISCLAIMER.md). This is not a
> sanctioned project.

This document outlines the cost assumptions and
phasing plan for AfriFlow.

## Cost Categories

- **Infrastructure**: compute, storage, networking.
- **Data engineering**: ingestion pipelines and
  transformations.
- **Platform operations**: monitoring, SRE, security.
- **Governance**: compliance, data stewardship, audits.

## Phasing

1. **Proof of Concept (PoC)**: core ingestion and one
   signal.
2. **Pilot**: expand to two additional domains, add
   entity resolution.
3. **Scale**: full five domain integration, country
   pod roll-out.

## Key Assumptions

- Cloud costs are estimated at 30% of total TCO.
- Data storage growth is projected at 20% year-over-year.
- Operational staffing includes 2 SREs, 3 data
  engineers, and 1 data steward.
